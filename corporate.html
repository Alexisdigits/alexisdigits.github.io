The Objective

Modern Corporate required a digital identity that reflected their transition into a "Data-First" company. The challenge was to take complex, cold enterprise information and present it through a human-centric, minimalist lens.

System Design

I developed a modular design system inspired by industrial blueprints. By prioritizing **white space** and **asymmetric grids**, we created a hierarchy that allows users to navigate high volumes of data without cognitive overload.
